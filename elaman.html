International Airport, Arabic. مطار حمد الدولي;
(IATA: DOH, ICAO: OTHH) is a civilian, one of the two airports in Qatar,
detained in the capital Doha. Built as a replacement for Doha Airport.
Formerly known as the New Doha Airport, Hamad Airport was scheduled
to open in 2009, however, after a series of long delays,
the airport opened on April 30, 2014. By May 27,
all aircraft of the national carrier Qatar Airways were transferred
to Hamad Airport. In 2020, the airport received the Skytrax
World Airport Awards as the best airport in the world with an annual passenger
flow of 30 to 40 million people, and also ranked third in the TOP 10
airports in the world
